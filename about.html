Our Story

  • ASEC Development & Construction Corporation was formally organized in May 1993. Projects initially undertaken were mostly minor renovations, interior fit-outs, and companct residences.

    To date, ASEC has gained a modest share of the industry's market by building industrial, institutional, recreational, commercial, medical, religious, infrastructure and other mixed-use projects in Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao. ASEC Development & Constructions Corporation is a AAA PCAB licensed General Contractor and is ISO 9001 Certified

    Accepting the flexibility and growth of the industry, the group has ventured into other related fields of technical expertise. Company resources have been expanded to engage in the following:

    The ASEC story presents the true ideals of the company's shareholders, mangement and staff. Not only is ASE committed towards the expansion of its business and resources, but it is focused on generating goodwill and professional Integration amond its peers, clients, the industry and the nation it wishes to serve.
